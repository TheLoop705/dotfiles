Terminal Shortcuts

WezTerm · tmux · zsh · Neovim — sultan@Ubuntu 26.04, from ~/.dotfiles

Ctrl+Alt+T → WezTerm → zsh → t → tmux / v or nvim → Neovim
Ctrl+Shift+Space is reserved system-wide for the Whisper dictation daemon (start/stop recording, works no matter what has focus). It is not a WezTerm, tmux, zsh, or Neovim shortcut — WezTerm explicitly disables its default QuickSelect binding on this combo and runs through XWayland so the daemon's global grab can see it.
