# Contributing

These are personal dotfiles for TheLoop705.
They are public for portability and reference, but the main job of this repo is to keep the owner's macOS and Ubuntu terminal workflow aligned.

## Bugs

For bugs, include:

- What happened.
- What you expected to happen.
- Steps to reproduce the problem.
- Your environment, including macOS, Linux, or WSL and any relevant Nix or home-manager generation details.

## Feature Ideas

Feature ideas should preserve the split between portable Home Manager config and macOS-only nix-darwin config.
